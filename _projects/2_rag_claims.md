Built and shipped multi-agent evaluation systems and RAG pipelines for insurance claims processing at **Tata AIA Life Insurance**.

## What it does

- Retrieves relevant policy clauses and historical claim decisions for any incoming claim
- Runs a multi-agent evaluator that grades extracted answers for faithfulness, completeness, and policy alignment
- Routes edge cases to human reviewers with structured rationale

## Impact

- Saved **thousands of person-hours** across claims operations
- Contributed directly to measurable business growth
- Used daily in a production environment

## Stack

`Python` · `LangChain` · `OpenAI` · `Vector DBs` · `FastAPI` · `Docker`
