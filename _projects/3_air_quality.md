Research project at **IIT Bombay** on forecasting urban air quality 3–5 days in advance for major Indian cities.

## Why

Growing up in India, pollution wasn't an abstract metric — it was something that shaped daily decisions. If meteorology can predict weather, why not air quality?

## What I built

- Time-series models combining meteorological inputs with historical PM2.5 / PM10 readings
- Multi-city forecasting with shared spatial features
- Comparative study of LSTM, Transformer, and ensemble approaches

## Stack

`Python` · `PyTorch` · `pandas` · `scikit-learn` · `NumPy`
