## üî¨ Current Research Interests

### 1. Agentic AI Systems

I'm fascinated by how we can make AI agents that:
- Collaborate with each other to solve complex problems
- Make autonomous decisions reliably
- Learn from their interactions and improve over time

**What I'm exploring:**
- Multi-agent frameworks (LangGraph, CrewAI)
- Agent orchestration patterns
- Reliability and safety in autonomous systems

**Recent Work:** Built a multi-agent evaluation platform that compares different LLMs (GPT-4, Claude, Gemini) with automated judging, achieving 89% correlation with human expert ratings.

### 2. Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)

RAG is revolutionizing how LLMs access and use external knowledge. I'm working on:
- Advanced chunking strategies for better context retrieval
- Hybrid search approaches (semantic + keyword)
- Re-ranking techniques to surface the most relevant information
- Building RAG systems that actually work in production

**Real Impact:** My RAG-based insurance chatbot helped customers understand complex policy documents by breaking them down intelligently and providing accurate, context-aware responses.

### 3. LLM Fine-Tuning & Optimization

Making large language models work for specific domains is both an art and science. I'm interested in:
- Parameter-efficient fine-tuning (QLORA, PEFT)
- Domain adaptation techniques
- Balancing accuracy, speed, and cost
- Evaluation methodologies for LLMs

**Experience:** Fine-tuned 5+ models (Llama 3.2, Qwen 2.5) for document classification, achieving 94% accuracy with significantly reduced inference costs.
